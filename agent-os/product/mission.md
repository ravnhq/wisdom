# Product Mission

## Problem

People get distracted while working and need a calmer, more inspiring environment that helps them refocus. Current browser experiences often make distractions one click away, while focus tools can feel separate from the daily workspace.

## Target Users

This product is for company knowledge workers, developers, managers, designers, and students who want help staying focused. It is especially useful for people who are easily distracted and need inspiration to make their browser feel like an environment where they want to work.

## Solution

Create a simple, holistic browser extension that combines focus mode, distraction blocking, nature-inspired new tab pages, and meaningful phrases. The extension makes inspiration part of the workflow by showing phrases on new tabs and blocked-site pages, explaining what each phrase means, and supporting custom inspiration from people with bright ideas inside the company.
